This command specifies the horizontal and vertical components of your vector plot.

Widget:

  • See Producing a Vector Plot.
Command:
  • VECTOR_COMPONENTS=hcomp, vcomp
Arguments:
  • hcomp: the horizontal component
  • vcomp: the vertical component
Examples:
  • VECTOR_COMPONENTS=U, W
  • VECTOR_COMPONENTS=D[T:X], D[T:Z]
Notes:
  • The vector components may be expressed either as a field variable, a function of field variables, or a diagnostic variable (see also FIELD).
