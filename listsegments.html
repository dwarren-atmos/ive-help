This command lets you get a list of the commands used to produce the most recent 15 plots.

Widget:

  • There is no widget selector for this command.
Command:
  • LISTSEGMENTS
Notes :
  • As more than 15 plots are made, the oldest commands are dropped off the list, and their segments are deleted from memory.
  • The list appears in the window that is running IVE, not in a pop-up window.
