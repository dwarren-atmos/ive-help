This command allows you to specify the pressure, temperature, and dew point components of a Skew T plot.

Widget:

  • (see Plotting a Temperature Profile on a Skew T, Log P Diagram
Command:
  • SKEWT_COMPONENTS= pressure, temperature, dew_point
Arguments :
  • pressure: the pressure component
  • temperature: the temperature component
  • dew_point: the dew point component (optional)
Examples :
  • SKEWT_COMPONENTS=PM, TC+273.16, TD+273.16
Notes :
  • The Skew T components may be expressed in tems of field variables, functions of field variables, or diagnostic variables (see also FIELD).
  • The dew point component is optional.
  • The pressure component must be in Pascal units (N/m^2) and the temperature and dew point components must be in Kelvins.
