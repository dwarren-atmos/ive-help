This command specifies the vector Maximum Value.

Widget:

  • From the vector Properties Window, enter the desired value in the "Maximum value" box (see Producing a Vector Plot).
Command:
  • VECTOR_MAX=value
Arguments:
  • value: the vector Maximum Value you wish to apply to your plot.
Examples:
  • VECTOR_MAX=45
Notes:
  • IVE assumes that the Maximum Value you enter is expressed in the same physical units as the magnitudes of the vectors in your field.
  • The Maximum Value scales all vectors in your field to the distance between plotted gridpoints, or scale distance. Thus, if the magnitude of a vector in your field exceeds the Maximum Value, its magnitude on the plot will appear larger than the scale distance. Conversely, if the magnitude of a vector is small compared to the Maximum Value, then its length will appear small.
