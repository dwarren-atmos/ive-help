This command specifies the widths of both major and minor tics.

Widget:

  • There is no widget interface for this command.
Command:
  • TIC_WIDTH=major, minor
Arguments:
  • major: the width of the major tics (thin, thick, or thickest)
  • minor: the width of the minor tics (thin, thick, or thickest)
Examples:
  • TIC_WIDTH=thick, thin
Notes:
  • To adjust the thickness of contours, see LINEWIDTH.
