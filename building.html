To build IVE from source, you need to have a version of make compatible with GNU Make. You also need version 3.2.1 or greater of NCAR Graphics library (note: NCAR graphics is NOT public domain - you must purchase a license), version 1.2.x of Motif library (which is included with most Unix OS), and the following public domain libraries:

  • NetCDF - version 3.3 or greater
  • udunits - version 1.10.8 or greater
  • xgks - version 2.5.5 with UW modifcations

The makefile assumes that the libraries have been installed in /usr/local/lib, and that the include files are in /usr/local/include. If they are in other locations you will have to edit the Makefile accordingly.

IVE has been successfully compiled from source for the following systems:

  • Sun 4 - SunOS 4.1.3
  • Sun - Solaris 2.5 and later with Sun unbundled compilers
  • DEC Alpha - Digital Unix 3.2C and later with DEC compilers
  • DEC MIPS RISC - Ultrix 4.3 with DEC compilers
  • SGI - IRIX 5.3 with SGI compilers

If you have a bug, or you wish to contribute other platforms to the IVE distribution, please send mail to ive-bugs@atmos.washington.edu.
